George Brown College, located in Toronto, Ontario, is a leading post-secondary institution that is a home for around 100,000 students. Established in 1987, the college offers a wide range of diploma, degree, and certificate programs across various fields such as
business, technology, health sciences, community services, and the arts. With its downtown campuses, St. James, Casa Loma, and Waterfront,
students have access to state-of-the-art facilities and close connections to industry partners, providing valuable
work-integrated learning opportunities. The college is well known for its focus on practical, hands-on education that prepares students for real-world careers.
