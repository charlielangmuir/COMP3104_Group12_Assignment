COMP 3104 DevOps course introduces students to automation, integration, and deployment processes.  
It focuses on bridging the gap between software development and operations teams.  
Students learn to use tools such as GitHub Actions for continuous integration and delivery.  
The course encourages frequent commits, version control, and collaboration in teams.  
By applying DevOps principles, teams can release software faster and with higher quality.  
It also emphasizes the importance of monitoring and feedback loops in the CI/CD pipeline.
