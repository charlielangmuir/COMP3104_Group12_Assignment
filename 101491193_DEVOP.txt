The COMP3104 DevOps course introduces students to the core principles of modern software delivery. 
It focuses on Continuous Integration (CI), Continuous Deployment (CD), automation, and collaboration between development and operations teams. 
Throughout the course, students learn how to use tools such as GitHub Actions, Docker, and pipelines to improve workflow efficiency. 
The main goal is to understand how DevOps reduces time-to-market while increasing product reliability and quality.
