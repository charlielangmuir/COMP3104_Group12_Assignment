George Brown College (GBC) is a leading applied arts and technology college located in Toronto, Canada. 
It provides students with hands-on experience through industry-focused programs and real-world projects.
The college emphasizes practical learning, teamwork, and innovation, helping students become job-ready 
in fields such as computer technology, business, design, and healthcare. 
Its downtown campuses make it easy for students to connect with companies and explore career opportunities.
