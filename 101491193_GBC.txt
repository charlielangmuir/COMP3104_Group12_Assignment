George Brown College is a leading applied arts and technology college located in Toronto, Canada.  
It provides a wide range of programs in computer technology, design, and business management.  
The college focuses on hands-on learning, ensuring students gain real-world experience.  
Many of its programs include co-op opportunities and project-based assessments.  
The campus environment is diverse, vibrant, and inclusive for students from all over the world.  
GBC aims to prepare students for future-ready careers in a rapidly changing digital world.
